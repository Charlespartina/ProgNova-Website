Contest Info

Everything you should know about ProgNova programming contest

Host

The 2nd ProgNova programming contest is organized and led by New York University Programming Team, with joint efforts from programming teams of other NY area universities.

Date and Time

The contest will be hosted online on Friday, October 14, 2016. 5-8pm EST.

Eligibility

There are two divisions for the contest:

• ICPC Division: Open to ICPC-eligible students this year from the participating universities.

• Open Division: Open to anyone from the participating universities, including alumni.

Rules

Standard ICPC rules with a few adjustments:

• The contest is individual

• The contest duration is 3 hours

Environment

Contestants use their own machines to compete and submit the solutions online to the judging platform. Access to local and online materials is allowed. However, contestants must not communicate or discuss the contest problems with any people during the contest.

Platform

The judging platform for ProgNova will be Kattis (open.kattis.com) or CodeChef (www.codechef.com)

Problem Settings

There'll be 7 problems in total. The expected difficulty distribution is: 1 Cakewalk, 2 Easy, 2 Medium, 2 Hard.

Prizes & Certificates

The two divisions will compete together. But the prizes and certificates will be awarded separately to the two divisions.

ICPC Division

• 1st place: a $100 amazon gift card.

• 2nd place: a $60 amazon gift card.

• 3rd place: a $30 amazon gift card.

• 3 lucky awards are drawn randomly from the top 20 finishers who are not in the top 3 in the ICPC Division. Each winner is awarded a $20 amazon gift card.

• Certificates will be awarded to the top 20 finishers.

Open Division

• 3 lucky awards are drawn randomly from the top 20 finishers who are not in the top 3 in the ICPC Division. Each winner is awarded a $20 amazon gift card.

• Certificates will be awarded to the top 20 finishers.
