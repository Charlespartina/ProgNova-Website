Prizes & Certificates

Show off your programming skills and win prizes and certificates!

The two divisions will compete together. Prizes will be awarded separately to the two divisions. Raffle prizes will be determined using this script.

Prize for ICPC Division

• 1st place: $150 Amazon gift card

• 2nd place: $100 Amazon gift card

• 3rd place: $50 Amazon gift card

• 3 lucky awards are drawn randomly from the top 10 finishers who are not in the top 3 in the ICPC Division. Each winner will receive a $20 Amazon gift card.

Prize for Open Division

• 3 lucky awards are drawn randomly from the top 10 finishers in the Open Division. Each winner is awarded one $20 Amazon gift card.

Certificates

• Certificates are awarded to the top 20 finishers of both divisions in electronic form upon requests.
