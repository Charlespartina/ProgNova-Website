The 2nd ProgNova Multi-University Programming Contest

Show off your competitive programming and algorithmic problem solving skills.

Date and Time

The contest will be hosted online on Friday, October 14, 2016. 5-8pm EST.

Participants

Students from universities participating ICPC from Greater New York Area are qualified for the ProgNova contest. All participants are separated into two divisions (ICPC division and Open division).

Prizes & Certificates

Prizes will be granted to winners and random participants in each divisions. Certificates will be granted to top 20 finishers.
